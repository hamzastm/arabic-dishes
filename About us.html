about us

We are 3 member team Wadee',Hamza and Naser. On our Project ,we offer a unique experience to explore ten of themost famous main dishes in Arabic cuisine. Each dish features a rich history, unique ingredients, andtraditional preparation methods.


    Each dish includes:

  • History of the Dish: A glimpse into its origin and development over time.
  • Detailed Ingredients: An accurate list of the ingredients you need to prepare the dish.
  • Preparation Method: A detailed explanation of each cooking step.
  • Serving Style: How to present the dish in a distinctive way.
  • Video Links: Tutorial videos to help you prepare the dishes professionally.
  • Famous Restaurants: Selected locations in Jordan that serve these traditional dishes.

    The Dishes We Offer:

  1. Mansaf (Jordan)
  2. Musakhan (Palestine)
  3. Koshary (Egypt)
  4. Kabsa (Saudi Arabia)
  5. Mandi (Yemen)
  6. Dolma (Iraq)
  7. Couscous (Tunisia)
  8. Mujadara (Lebanon)
  9. Molokhia (Egypt)
  10. Stuffed Grape Leaves (Levant)

Enjoy the experience of Arabic cuisine through our website and discover how you can easily prepare these delicious dishes at home!
