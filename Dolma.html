Dolma (Iraq)


Dish History:

Dolma is one of the famous dishes in Iraqi cuisine, as well as in most of the Levant and Turkey. Its history dates back to ancient times, when it was primarily prepared for royal families and nobles. It is considered an ideal dish for special occasions.


    Ingredients:

  • 1 kg minced meat (usually lamb or beef)
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 1 cup pine nuts
  • 1 tsp spices
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Grape leaves, zucchini, or eggplant for stuffing

Preparation Method:

  1. Preparing the Filling: In a large bowl, mix the minced meat with rice and chopped onions. Add the spices and pine nuts, then combine thoroughly.
  2. Stuffing the Vegetables: Stuff the grape leaves, zucchini, or eggplant with the meat and rice mixture.
  3. Preparing the Broth: In a large pot, heat some ghee and add the tomato paste. Pour in water and bring it to a boil. Place the stuffed dolma in the broth and cover the pot.
  4. Cooking the Dolma: Let it simmer on low heat for 45-60 minutes until the vegetables are tender and fully cooked.

Serving Method:

Dolma is served with lemon juice and yogurt on the side.
